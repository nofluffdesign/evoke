Management | Production | Finance

Management

Evoke represents top-tier showrunners, show creators, writers, directors, producers, and production companies in film, TV, and new media.

Production

Evoke has produced and financed several series and movies since its inception, including the hit series Creepshow for Shudder/AMC, the animated family series Twelve Forever for Netflix and the Universal/Syfy series Day of the Dead. Evoke also produced the series Ties That Bind for Up TV, and A Place in the Sun for Discovery.

In 2025 Evoke is producing the 26 episode unscripted series Forever Young with Dr. Pablo Prichard for NBC, the adult animated series Breaking Bear for Tubi starring Academy Award Winner Brendan Fraser, Sarah Michelle Gellar, Josh Gad, Annie Murphy and Elizabeth Hurley, approximately 10 television movies for networks such as HallMark Channel, Lifetime Networks and Tubi, and 4 independent movies through its partnership with Magenta Light Studios.

Evoke also has a deal to produce 20 TV Movies in 2025 and 2026 which it is producing and financing in Buffalo, NY and Winnipeg Canada. Evoke is also rendering Production services for several projects including Netflix and CBS Studios upcoming reboot of Little House on the Prairie and has multiple series and feature films in development at Netflix and other major networks and streamers.

Finance

Evoke is a full-service finance company and studio. CEO Stan Spry has been responsible for putting together nearly a quarter of a billion dollars in financing over the last decade for movies and television shows, including Breaking Bear (Tubi), Forever Young (NBC/Peacock), Creepshow (AMC/Shudder), Day of the Dead (SyFy/Universal), 12 Forever (Netflix), the 20 Picture Buffalo and Winnipeg deal, and many more.
